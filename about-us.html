Meet Your Therapist

Hi, I'm James – a holistic therapist with a deep passion for helping people reconnect with themselves—body, mind, and spirit.

Before becoming a therapist, I worked in family support for 12 years. During that time, I witnessed many families struggle, and it inspired me to help people on a deeper, more personal level. As a father, I also came to understand that my own wellbeing directly influences the wellbeing of my daughters—and that real change starts from within.

In 2016, a transformative trip to Peru led me to a spiritual healer who helped me do some powerful inner work. She told me that my path was to learn different ways of healing and eventually teach others too. This message stayed with me.

At the time, I was already a qualified Reiki therapist. I followed that calling and became a Reiki Master. I soon realised that people often need support in more than one area—so I expanded my practice by training in massage therapy, specialising in sports and deep tissue techniques.

Today, I bring together physical, emotional, and energetic healing to help clients feel more balanced, grounded, and empowered. I’m a registered member of the Federation of Holistic Therapists (FHT) and I’m always learning, growing, and deepening my skills to support those I work with.
